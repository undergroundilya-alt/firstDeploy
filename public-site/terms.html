1. Service overview

AdProof provides ad visibility verification tools for publishers and website owners. The service may include a browser SDK, server-side verification, heartbeat checks, domain validation, event analytics, dashboards, and reporting tools.

2. Beta trial

The free beta trial is provided for testing and evaluation. Trial limits, such as number of projects, domains, daily visits, or ad visibility checks, may be changed to protect service stability or prevent abuse.

3. Account responsibilities

You are responsible for providing accurate account information, keeping login credentials secure, and making sure that AdProof is installed only on websites you own, operate, or are authorized to manage.

4. Acceptable use

You may not use AdProof for illegal activity, deceptive traffic generation, ad fraud, malicious tracking, unauthorized monitoring, attempts to bypass platform security, or any activity that violates applicable laws, ad network rules, or third-party platform policies.

5. Publisher and ad network compliance

You are responsible for complying with the policies of your advertising partners, including Google AdSense, Google Ad Manager, or other networks you use. AdProof is intended to measure and verify ad visibility conditions, not to manipulate ad reporting or create invalid traffic.

6. Data and analytics

AdProof may process technical events such as page URL, domain, project identifier, visitor/session token, ad visibility status, heartbeat status, browser signals, and integration health events. These events are used for security, analytics, debugging, billing, and service improvement.

7. Service availability

The beta service is provided as-is and may be changed, limited, interrupted, or discontinued during testing. We may suspend access if usage threatens platform stability, exceeds trial limits, or appears abusive.

9. Limitation of liability
