# Legal and commercial launch checklist

This checklist is for planning and does not replace legal advice.

## Before paid production launch

- Privacy Policy.
- Terms of Service.
- Data Processing Agreement / DPA where needed.
- Cookie/consent review for target markets.
- Clear publisher-facing integration terms.
- Security contact email.
- Incident response process.
- Data retention policy.
- Backup and restore policy.
- SLA/support scope for beta vs paid clients.
- Abuse/appeal flow for false positives.

## Product transparency

Use clear messaging:

- what is being checked;
- why protected content may be delayed;
- what the user can do when the overlay appears;
- how connection issues differ from filtering/blocking.

## Commercial positioning

Describe the product as ad visibility verification and protected-content access control, not as a guarantee of defeating every browser extension forever.
Browsers, extensions and rulesets change over time.
