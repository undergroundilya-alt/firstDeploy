# 16. Beta limitations

This beta is intentionally practical and understandable. It is not marketed as unbreakable protection.

## Limitations

- JSON storage is not suitable for high traffic.
- Frontend-only soft-gate can be bypassed by advanced users.
- Real ad networks may have their own policies and integration limitations.
- Browser extensions can evolve.
- False positives must be tested carefully.
- Real customer rollout needs privacy/legal review.

## Correct promise

Do not promise “we block all adblockers forever”.

Better promise:

“We verify advertising visibility, classify blocking scenarios, provide analytics and can connect protected content access to ad visibility confirmation.”
